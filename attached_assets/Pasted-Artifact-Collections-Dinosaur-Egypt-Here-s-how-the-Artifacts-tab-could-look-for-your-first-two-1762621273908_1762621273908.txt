Artifact Collections (Dinosaur & Egypt)
Here's how the "Artifacts" tab could look for your first two destinations. Remember, each artifact gives a small permanent boost (like +0.5% All Revenue), and completing the set gives a big bonus.

Destination: Dinosaur Era (65 Million BC)
Collection: "Relics of the Cretaceous"

Artifacts to Find:

Polished Amber: (Common) A piece of hardened sap with a tiny, trapped fly.

Velociraptor Claw: (Common) A single, curved claw.

Ginkgo Leaf Fossil: (Uncommon) An imprint of an ancient leaf, perfectly preserved.

Triceratops Horn Fragment: (Uncommon) The chipped-off point of a massive horn.

Pristine T-Rex Tooth: (Rare) A flawless, giant tooth.

Set Bonus: "Primal Fury"

Effect: The "Click Boost!" button is now 25% more powerful.

Destination: Ancient Egypt (2500 BC)
Collection: "Treasures of the Nile"

Artifacts to Find:

Clay Scarab Amulet: (Common) A small beetle charm, said to bring good luck.

Hieroglyph Tablet Scrap: (Common) A fragment of stone with "Time Machine" etched on it... how?

Linen Mummy Wrapping: (Uncommon) A surprisingly well-preserved strip of cloth.

Golden Ankh: (Uncommon) A beautiful symbol of life.

Eye of Horus Pendant: (Rare) A glowing amulet, pulsing with faint energy.

Set Bonus: "Pharaoh's Blessing"

Effect: All Managers now cost 10% less to hire and upgrade.

2. The "Portal" Visuals (A Fantastic Idea!)
This adds so much visual flair! The central time machine stays the same, but the "portal" effect that gathers customers and shoots the machine off-world should change based on the active destination.

Default (The Present): The glowing green, high-tech ring you have now.

Dinosaur Era Portal: A "Jungle Portal" — a swirling vortex of green leaves, vines, and cracked earth.

Ancient Egypt Portal: A "Sandstorm Portal" — a vortex of golden sand and shimmering desert heat.

This makes every destination feel truly unique, not just a menu item.

3. More Destination Ideas
Let's add more stops to the timeline! Each one can have its own "Base Fare," unique portal, and Artifact collection.

The Wild West (Frontier Town, 1870 AD)

Description: "Stake your claim in an era of outlaws and gold!"

Base Fare: 2,500

Portal: A "Dust Devil Portal" that looks like a swirling tumbleweed or a mini-tornado of dust.

Artifacts: Sheriff's Badge, Gold Nugget, Saloon Token, Wanted Poster, (Rare) Ornate Six-Shooter.

Set Bonus: "Gold Rush" — 5% chance any trip pays out 10x ChronoCoins.

Cyberpunk City (Neo-Kyoto, 2242 AD)

Description: "Navigate the neon-drenched streets of a high-tech future."

Base Fare: 500,000

Portal: A "Digital Rift Portal" — a glitchy, neon-blue matrix of 1s and 0s and flickering code.

Artifacts: Glitching Data-Chip, Cybernetic Eye, Flying 'Noodle' Ad, Corporate Security Badge, (Rare) Sentient AI Core.

Set Bonus: "System Overclock" — All ad-boosts (2x Revenue, etc.) last 30 seconds longer.

Age of Sail (Tortuga, 1701 AD)

Description: "Seek treasure and adventure on the high seas!"

Base Fare: 75,000

Portal: A "Maelstrom Portal" — a swirling, dark-blue whirlpool of seawater and sea foam.

Artifacts: Piece of Eight, Pirate's Compass, Jolly Roger Flag, Message in a Bottle, (Rare) Black Pearl.

Set Bonus: "Buried Treasure" — Once per day, a "Treasure Chest" appears. Clicking it gives a massive bundle of ChronoCoins (e.g., 1 hour of earnings).
