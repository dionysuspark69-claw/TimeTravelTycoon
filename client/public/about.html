What is ChronoTransit?

ChronoTransit is a free-to-play idle incremental game that runs entirely in your web browser. You play as the owner of a time travel transportation company. Your job is to board customers onto your time machines, send them to historical destinations, and collect ChronoCoins as payment.

As you earn more, you can upgrade your time machines to carry more passengers, travel faster, and generate more revenue per trip. Hire specialist managers with unique perks, discover rare temporal artifacts, complete missions, and eventually prestige to unlock permanent multipliers that carry across resets.

The game runs while you are away. Close the tab, come back later, and collect your offline earnings. No energy systems, no paywalls, no downloads. Just a pure idle game in your browser.

How to Play

Getting started: When you first open ChronoTransit, customers will begin arriving at your time machine depot. Click to send them on trips and collect ChronoCoins when they return.

Upgrading: Spend your ChronoCoins in the Upgrades tab to level up your time machine, increase passenger capacity, boost trip speed, and improve customer arrival rates. Each upgrade compounds on the previous one.

Managers: Once you have enough coins, hire managers from the Managers tab. Each manager has a unique upgrade tree with perks that automate and enhance your operation. The Recruiter brings more customers, the Technician speeds up trips, and the Accountant multiplies your income.

Prestige: When you have earned 50 million ChronoCoins, reached Time Machine Level 25, and own 5 machines, you can prestige. This resets your progress but grants you a permanent revenue multiplier that makes every future run faster and more profitable. Each subsequent prestige requires more to unlock but stacks indefinitely.

Offline progress: ChronoTransit keeps earning while you are away. Up to 4 hours of offline earnings are calculated automatically when you return. Sign in with Google to cloud-save your progress and access it from any device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChronoTransit free to play?

Yes, ChronoTransit is completely free to play with no purchase required. You can optionally watch ads for temporary in-game boosts, but all game content is accessible without spending money.

Do I need to download anything?

No. ChronoTransit runs entirely in your web browser. Just visit timetraveltycoon.onrender.com and start playing instantly.

How do I save my progress?

Sign in with Google using the Account tab in the Settings menu. Your progress is automatically saved to the cloud every 15 seconds and can be restored on any device.

What is the prestige system?

Prestiging resets your coins and upgrades but grants a permanent revenue multiplier. Each prestige requires progressively higher goals, but the stacking multipliers make the game faster and more rewarding with each cycle.

Does the game work on mobile?

Yes, ChronoTransit is designed to work on both desktop and mobile browsers. The interface adapts to smaller screens automatically.
